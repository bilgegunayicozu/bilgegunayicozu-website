Statement

My practice emerges from a growing desire to highlight the raw, often overlooked materials and phenomenon embedded in our everyday lives. I work with reality as it is: revealing structures that usually remain hidden, unnoticed or discarded. I am drawn to functional and perceptual tensions, and to aesthetics that emerge not from ornament but from presence, process and structure.

Rather than using technology only as a tool, I treat it as a subject in itself - something to be listened to, observed and questioned. What happens when we strip technology of its sleek interfaces and allow its inner workings to become visible, audible and performative? This approach leads me to work with found or industrial materials, electronic circuitry, and systems that exist outside conventional artistic framing.

Bio

I'm an artist and producer based in Valencia, Spain. My work moves between sound-light installation, audiovisual and immersive experiences, generative/real time/interactive systems that explores the material and sonic qualities of technology and making visible what usually remains hidden.

Since 2019, I've been developing a parallel practice as one half of generative art duo dist.cs, working with Ilgın İçözü on rule-based generative systems that produce evolving visual and sonic compositions. Our work has been exhibited internationally at Art Basel Miami Beach (with the Tezos Foundation), Proof of People in New York, and in galleries and festivals across Europe, Turkey, and the United States.

I am also a member of Archivo Cero, an interdisciplinary collective researching the intersection of technology and perception through interactive systems and immersive sensory environments - presented most recently as Col·lapse at Bombas Gens, Valencia.

Alongside my artistic practice, I have over eight years of experience in live music production, artist management and international touring: coordinating artists, technical crews, venues, and multidisciplinary teams for events across Turkey and Europe. This background shapes how I approach everything I make and everything I produce: with close attention to what it actually takes to realise ambitious work in real conditions.

I hold a Bachelor's in Arts and Cultural Management from Istanbul Bilgi University, a Master's in Music from Istanbul Technical University (MIAM), and a Master's in Multimedia Creation for Live Shows from LABA Valencia (2025).
